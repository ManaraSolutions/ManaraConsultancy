How we help you show up

Personal styling

Personalised advice based on your preferences, body type and the occasions you dress for. We help you select outfits that suit your style and shape, and teach you how to make stylish choices independently.

Wardrobe consultation & audit

We review and organise your existing wardrobe, build a style-and-measurement profile, identify what is working and what is missing, and turn it into a plan of outfits you can actually wear.

Personal shopping

We find the pieces that complete your looks — sourcing specific items on request, recommending accessories and footwear, and saving you the hours and the mistakes.

Trend & colour advice

We stay current on trends, designer collections and industry shifts, and translate them for you through colour theory and body shape — so what you buy is both on-trend and genuinely right for you.

Corporate & executive styling

For leaders and public-facing professionals: a signature style and a wardrobe that reads as credible and cohesive, reinforcing personal brand and the psychological impact of appearance.

Event & occasion styling

Coordinated looks for events, photoshoots and public appearances — outfit, accessories and footwear pulled together so you arrive camera-ready and at ease.

Virtual consultations

Can't meet in person? We run styling sessions, wardrobe reviews and shopping guidance virtually, with a shared style profile and lookbook you keep.

Style education

Beyond a single makeover, we teach the fundamentals — proportion, palette and seasonal dressing — so you leave able to make confident, independent choices.

Building a personal brand as well as a wardrobe? Pair your styling with LinkedIn & personal branding so how you look and how you're seen tell one story.

Frequently asked questions

Who is fashion consulting for?

Everyone from high-profile clients and business executives seeking a more sophisticated wardrobe to everyday individuals wanting to refresh their personal style. We tailor advice to your preferences, body type, lifestyle and the context you move in.

What happens in a wardrobe consultation?

We audit what you already own, organise it, identify gaps, and build a plan of outfits and additions that work for your body, your colours and your calendar. We can follow it with personal shopping to fill the gaps.

Do you offer virtual consultations?

Yes. For clients who cannot meet in person we run styling sessions, wardrobe reviews and shopping guidance virtually, with a shared style profile and lookbook.

Can you style for a specific event or photoshoot?

Yes. We coordinate outfits, accessories and footwear for events, photoshoots and public appearances, and can source specific pieces on request.

How much does it cost?

It depends on the service, scope and whether it is a one-off session or ongoing styling. Use the fashion styling form to describe what you need and we return a tailored quotation.
