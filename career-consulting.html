Frequently asked questions

Who is career coaching for?

Professionals at any level facing a real decision: a move, a promotion, a relocation, a return to work or a change of field. Sessions are one-to-one and confidential.

Can you rewrite my CV?

Yes. CV development is a standalone service: structure, wording and keyword optimisation for applicant tracking systems, in English, French or Arabic.

Do you prepare people for interviews?

Yes. We run realistic mock interviews for your specific role and sector, then work on the answers, stories and salary negotiation.

Do you advise on Gulf relocation?

Yes. We advise on Gulf market entry for individuals: which markets suit your profile, what packages look like and how to land interviews from abroad.
