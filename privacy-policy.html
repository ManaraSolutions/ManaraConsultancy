Privacy Policy.

This policy explains what information Manara Consultancy collects, why, and how we protect it. It applies to this website and our advisory services.

Information we collect

We collect information you choose to give us: your name, email, phone or WhatsApp number, company and the details of your inquiry when you contact us, request a consultation or use the assistant on this site.

We collect basic technical data such as browser type and pages visited, used only to keep the site working and to improve it.

How we use it

To respond to your inquiry, prepare and deliver services, send you information you requested, and confirm bookings and payments. We do not sell your personal information to anyone.

Confidentiality

All discussions and documents shared with us are treated as strictly confidential. Non-disclosure agreements are available on request for business and academic clients.

Data retention & your rights

We keep personal data only as long as needed for the purpose it was given or as required by law. You may ask us to access, correct or delete your information at any time by contacting us through this site.

Third parties

We use trusted providers for messaging, payments and analytics, each bound by their own privacy terms. Payment details are handled by the payment provider, not stored by us.

Contact

Questions about this policy can be sent through the contact options on our homepage. This policy may be updated; the current version always appears on this page.
