How we help you show up as an employer

Organisational design

Clear roles, structures and reporting lines — so accountability is obvious and the right work reaches the right seat. We turn an implicit org into an intentional one.

Recruiting & selection

From a real job definition to structured sourcing, screening and selection — a bias‑aware recruiting process, so you stop hiring for the seat you have and start hiring for the seat you actually need.

HR policies & handbooks

The rules, written down — leave, conduct, remote work, grievance and more — consistent, fair, and aligned to Lebanese labour law. No more policy living in someone's head.

Performance & development

Simple, honest frameworks for goals, reviews and growth — so feedback is regular, expectations are shared, and people can see a path forward.

Compensation, benefits & insurance

Pay and benefit structures that are fair internally and competitive externally — salary bands, health insurance, NSSF and end‑of‑service indemnity, and clear progression, with far fewer awkward conversations.

Diversity, equity & multicultural teams

Inclusive hiring and team design across cultures, languages and generations — turning the multicultural talent of Lebanon, MENA and the Gulf into a genuine advantage rather than a source of friction.

Culture & engagement

Retention, onboarding and employer brand — the deliberate work of making people want to join, stay and do their best. Culture as a system, not a slogan.

Employment & compliance

Contracts and HR compliance in Arabic, English and French, aligned to Lebanese labour law — with our Legal practice on hand when a matter needs formal counsel.

Fractional HR partner

For teams too small for a full HR function: a steady, senior hand on retainer — the people side handled deliberately, without the overhead of a hire.

Frequently asked questions

What does an HR consultancy actually do?

We make the people side of a business as deliberate as its strategy: defining roles and structure, hiring the right person for the right seat, writing the policies that usually live in someone's head, and building fair performance and pay frameworks — all compliant with Lebanese labour law.

We're a small team — is this for us?

Especially. Small teams feel a mis‑hire or an unclear role hardest. We right‑size everything to your stage, so you get structure without bureaucracy — including a fractional HR partner if a full function isn't yet warranted.

Can you help with one problem rather than a full retainer?

Yes. Engagements range from a single piece of work — an employee handbook, an org chart, a hiring process — to ongoing HR partnership. Tell us the problem and we scope it honestly.

Do you handle employment contracts and compliance?

Yes, in Arabic, English and French, aligned to Lebanese labour law — and where a matter needs formal legal counsel we coordinate with our Legal practice.

How much does it cost?

It depends on scope and whether it's a one‑off or ongoing. Use the consultation form to describe what you need and we return an honest assessment and a fee proposal.
